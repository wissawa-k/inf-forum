    About Inf-Forum

    Inf-Forum is a project that explores a different way of learning by presenting educational content in a familiar feed. Instead of reading through long articles, users can quickly discover interesting facts about history, science, technology, and more through bite-sized posts inspired by everyday browsing experiences.

    Why we built it

    Most platforms today are designed to keep people scrolling, but not necessarily learning. Inf-Forum takes that same familiar browsing experience and uses it to encourage curiosity, helping users discover something new each time they open the app.

    Why it matters

    • Makes educational content easier to approach.
    • Encourages curiosity and self-directed learning.
    • Lightweight static website with no dedicated backend required.
    • Easy and inexpensive to deploy.

    Sustainable Development Goal

    SDG 4 - Quality Education

    Inf-Forum supports Quality Education by making learning more accessible, engaging, and easier to explore through modern web technologies.
